Research Interests

Internet scale systems; networked distributed systems; collaborative systems; Peer-to-Peer systems; economics based algorithms and systems applied to distributed systems; collaborative learning and networking.

Short Bio

Leandro Navarro joined the Department of Computer Architecture of UPC as a research support student in 1985, and as an associate professor in 1988 after receiving his graduate degree on Telecommunication Engineering from UPC. He received his Ph.D. from UPC in 1992.
