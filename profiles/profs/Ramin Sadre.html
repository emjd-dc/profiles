Short Bio

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department for Computer Science (INGI) at the Université catholique de Louvain. Before that, I worked in the Distributed and Embedded Systems (DES) group at Aalborg University and at the chair Design and Analysis of Communication Systems (DACS) of the University of Twente (UT). My research interests include the design and analytical performance evaluation of communication systems and distributed systems, the design of network intrusion detection systems, and security in industrial control networks and IoT.
