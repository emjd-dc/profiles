Short Bio

Felix Freitag is associate Professor at the Department of Computer Architecture of the Technical University of Catalonia (UPC) in Barcelona. His current research interests include peer-to-peer and cloud computing, with application to community contexts. He received his PhD in Telecommunication Engineering in 1998. For further details contact him at felix@ac.upc.edu.
